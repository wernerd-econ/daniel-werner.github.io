Hello! I am a predoctoral research assistant at Stanford University, working in the Gentzkow Lab at the Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research (SIEPR).

My research interests lie in applied microeconomics, with a focus on political economy and the effects of organized crime and civil conflict in developing economies.

My independent research examines the effect of organized crime on educational outcomes in Mexico and political alignment biases in federal disaster aid allocation.
